Our Story

Built by someone who needed it

Ghost Logic LLC was founded by Christopher Green in Hope Mills, NC. After years of watching AI tools get built exclusively for Fortune 500 companies with million-dollar budgets, we decided to change that.

The idea is simple: what if a regular family had access to the same AI-powered tools as a major corporation? What if a small business owner could automate the same workflows that Amazon uses? What if your household ran as efficiently as a well-managed company?

That is what Ghost Logic builds. Not hype, not demos — working systems that make your life measurably better.
