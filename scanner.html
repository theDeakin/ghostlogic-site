What You Get

Built to give you an edge

Every field in the report means something. No filler, no padding — just the data that moves trades.

Scan Score (1-10)

Proprietary composite score weighing gap magnitude, relative volume, sector momentum, and signal quality. 7+ is worth watching. 8+ is a setup.

Gap % with Direction

Pre-market gap size relative to prior close. Green for gap-up, red for gap-down. Includes context on whether the gap is supported by volume.

Relative Volume (RVOL)

Current pre-market volume versus 20-day average for the same time window. RVOL above 2x means real participation. 4x+ is high-conviction.

Entry / Stop / Target

Exact levels derived from pre-market structure and prior-day key zones. Not guesses — levels that respect the chart. Risk-defined before the open.

Sector Momentum

Which sectors are leading and lagging pre-market. Knowing sector tailwinds before entry dramatically improves trade conviction and accuracy.

Signal Classification

Every setup is typed: Breakout, Momentum, Reversal, Gap Fill, or Catalyst. Helps you instantly know the trade thesis without reading paragraphs.

Earnings & Catalyst Flags
